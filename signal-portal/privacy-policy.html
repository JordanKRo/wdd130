Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 7, 2024

Signal Portal (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and store data collected through our app for the purpose of conducting a local study on internet speed and connectivity in the area.

1. Information We Collect

Signal Portal collects the following data automatically and anonymously:

  • Device Information: This includes diagnostics such as battery level and status, as well as network information.
  • Network Information: The app collects the MAC address and host name of Wi-Fi access points and cell towers to which the device connects.
  • Internet Speed: We measure upload and download speeds for both Wi-Fi and cellular connections.
  • Precise Location (GPS): To generate a geographic heatmap of signal quality, we collect GPS coordinates of the device.

2. How We Use Collected Information

The data collected by Signal Portal is used solely for research purposes, specifically to analyze internet speed and connectivity patterns in the local area. The data will help us identify trends and create publicly accessible maps showing general connectivity information.

Note: Publicly accessible data does not include MAC addresses or other identifying network information.

3. Data Anonymization and Security

Signal Portal does not collect any personal information, such as names, email addresses, or contact information. All data is anonymized before it is stored and analyzed, ensuring individual devices cannot be linked back to specific users. We implement strict security measures to protect all data collected from unauthorized access.

4. Data Retention

Collected data is stored only as long as necessary to conduct our research and generate connectivity reports. Data will be deleted when it is no longer required.

5. Location Permissions

Signal Portal requires access to location services in order to collect accurate data on connectivity in your area. This permission is only used for the purposes stated and is not shared with any third parties.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@signal.org.

By using Signal Portal, you consent to the collection and use of data as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
