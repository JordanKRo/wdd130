What is Signal Portal?

Signal portal is a Study being conducted on the BYUI campus to find area's, times, networks, and devices provide the best internet access on campus and out. On Wifi and on Cell networks.

Participants start by installing the app on their mobile device. Once configured the app provides network speed telemetry to the Signal Portal data base. It's that easy, if data rates or battery is a concern, the app can be configured to meter the usage of your battery and cell data. Any contribution no matter the size is valuable to the open source database.

Help us by installing the app and contributing to the Data Base. It's fast and any little bit helps
